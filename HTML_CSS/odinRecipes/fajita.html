Fajita Recipe

Fajitas are a traditional Mexican Food which is enjoyed all over the world

Ingredients

  • Paprika
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Wraps
  • Acovado

Steps

  1. Cook the Seasoned Chicken
  2. Take the chicken out of the pan and cook the thinly cut paprika and onion
  3. Put the Chicken back in the Pan
  4. let it cook for about 4min
  5. Take it out of the Pan and put it inside the warap, enjoy
